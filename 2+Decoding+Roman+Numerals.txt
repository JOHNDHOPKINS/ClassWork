
2 Decoding Roman Numerals

summary: a program that can convert Roman numerals to their equal number value.

Input:
I  V  X
Output:
I translated to 1
V translated to 5
X translated to 10

Input:
L  C  II
Output:
L translated to 50
C translated to 100
II translated to 2

Input:
III  IV  VI
Output:
III translated to 3
IV translated to 4
VI translated to 6

Input:
VII  IIX  IX
Output:
VII translated to 7
IIX translated to 8
IX translated to 9

Input:
CL  CX  CV
Output:
CL translated to 150
CX translated to 110
CV translated to 105

Input:
CI  LX  LV
Output:
CI translated to 101
LX translated to 60
LV translated to 55

Input:
LI  CVII  CLXVI
Output:
LI translated to 51
CVII translated to 107
CLXVI translated to 166
